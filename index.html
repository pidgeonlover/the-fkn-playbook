Why Now

Three things broke at once.

The default distribution playbook of the last decade (SEM, SEO, PPC, email) is bleeding out. Paid acquisition has become a tax that compounds against you, not for you. Here's why this playbook only works right now.

01

Paid acquisition collapsed

CACs up, ad relevance down, ROAS shrinking across nearly every B2B and B2C category since 2020. Meta and Google now charge you more to reach fewer of the right people. The math stopped working.

02

Building got 100x cheaper

Anyone with Cursor, Lovable, or Claude Code can ship a product in a weekend. Supply of products is exploding. Demand for distribution is unchanged. Code is a commodity. Distribution is the moat.

03

Creators went mainstream

LinkedIn, X, TikTok, and YouTube are now the primary discovery layer for B2B and B2C alike. Operators who engineer their own creator economy eat the distribution that used to belong to ad budgets.

Miss this wave and you're obsolete by 2028. Catch it and you build the next category leaders. This playbook has a 3–5 year shelf life. The clock is running.
